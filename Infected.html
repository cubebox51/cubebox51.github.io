How to play: Your goal as a virus is to infect the whole body as you go but you must watch out for white blood cells and obstacles as you go.

Controls: Use number keys to select options on what to do.

Mucus Instructions

1. First you must attack the mucus by answering the question correctly

2. Then hit the "1" key when the pointer is on the "100" to do more damage

3. Repeat untill dead.

Cell Invading Instructions

1. Choose the correct protien to attatch to by selecting the number above it.

2. Answer a question correctly to inject yourself in.

3. Press the key that it tells you to hyjacks the cell.

4. Franticly tap "1" key to fill up the bar and produce more viruses.

5. Press "2" to exit the cell.

White Blood Cell Instructions

1. First you must attack the white blood cell by answering the question correctly

2. Then hit the "1" key when the pointer is on the "100" to do more damage

3. Repeat untill dead.
